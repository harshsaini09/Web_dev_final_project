about Shruti

I am from Dayalbagh, Agra. A place of beauty and nature. Since my childhood, i love art and design. I always try to design stuff with my unique point of view. I also love to create things that can be usefull to others.

I started coding since I was in high school. Coding is also an art for me. I love it and now I have the opportunity to design along with the coding. I find it really interesting and I enjoyed the process a lot.

My vision is to make the world a better place. Now almost everything is becoming better than ever. It is time for us to create more good stuff that helps the world to become a better place.

So my life started like this,I was born on 25 September 2001 at Jharkhand.I have done my High School in 2017 and completed my intermediate in 2019 from D.E.I. Prem Vidyalaya Girls Intermediate College, Dayalbagh, Agra. Currently,I am pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science specialization from Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra. Socialising with friends and family, painting,Playing badminton,reading books,solving puzzles and computing are my hobbies. Good communication skills,critical thinking , working well in a team, self-motivation,good time management are my well known abilities.
